Hyperion

The Cultural Society

Hyperion- The Cultural Society of PGDAV College brings together more than 12 different societies belonging to various genres. From western dance to classical, from technical to beatboxing, you name it we have it. Even though we follow diverse passions, but at the end of the day we are ONE and Hyperion is the Glue that binds all the societies together.

Here at Hyperion, we believe in the growth of the community as well as the individual. We have collaborated with some of the best personas in their respective fields.

From EDM concerts to Kavi Samelans, from Standup Comedians to Punjabi Artists. Hyperion- The Cultural Society of PGDAV College strived to provide the college students the best experiences. If events are ART, then we are the ARTISTS.
